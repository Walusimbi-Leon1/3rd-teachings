# 3rd Teachings — Philosophical Wisdom

**Philosophical Wisdom** by Leon (SGSS) — the third volume in the Spiritual Growth & Spiritual Science series.

This book is a collection of philosophical teachings (LPT — Leon's Philosophical Teachings) spanning from October 2025 onward. Each entry challenges conventional thinking, explores the nature of the mind, emotions, perception, and the human condition with raw honesty and sharp insight.

## Topics Covered

- The nature of the mind and perception
- Cues, cravings, and rewards (CCR)
- Emotions and how they shape reality
- Personality (PS) and staying true to your purpose
- The relationship between humans, animals, and the Gods
- The Thirteen Files of Leon (TTFL)
- Death, praise, and the meaning of life
